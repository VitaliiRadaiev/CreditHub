Our other Services

  • Subdivision

    If subdividing your land is on your mind, we’re here to help. Whether it is liaising with your surveyor and lender or preparing the appropriate documentation for lodging the plan with the Lands Title Office, we are there to take total control of the process so that you can sit back and relax.

  • Transfer of ownership

    New partners, separations, refinancing and liability issues are common reasons for why a Title of a property might need to be altered. Our conveyancer is well equipped to assist and make sure there will be a smooth transfer of ownership between both parties.

  • Business Conveyancing

    If you are planning to purchase or sell a business, professional legal advice is crucial. Our conveyancers are well-equipped with a thorough knowledge of Australian business laws. We take care of all your needs, starting from the Contract of Sale of the business to Settlement, and guide you through the entire process.

  • Commercial Property Sale and Purchase

    Commercial conveyancing is not the same as that of residential properties and that’s why you require the expert advice. We ensure maximum legal protection as we follow the process – prepare the Contract of Purchase, procure all pre-purchase documents, negotiate on your behalf, secure Exchange of Contracts, and finally reach Settlement.
